About Us

Drift & Co was born during the quiet chaos of the pandemic — a time when movement was limited, but creativity had no brakes. Fueled by a passion for street culture and self-expression, we created a brand that captures the spirit of the streets: bold, raw, and unapologetically real.

We specialize in printed streetwear designed for individuals who live fast, stand out, and wear their energy. Every piece reflects drift culture, late-night drives, and underground style — made for those who move with purpose and passion.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through expressive streetwear that embodies motion, attitude, and authenticity. We aim to connect with those who live fast, speak loud, and wear what they feel.

Our Vision

To become a leading streetwear brand in the Philippines that represents youth culture, creativity, and fearless individuality — where every piece tells a story and every thread moves with purpose.
